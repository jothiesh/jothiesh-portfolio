About me.

A short brief — who I am, where I work, and the kind of problems I want to solve next.

I started writing Java as a junior dev in 2022. Two years later I was the sole engineer architecting a fleet platform handling 20,000 concurrent devices in production.

At Thinture Technologies I own seven end-to-end platforms — from system design and Spring Boot backends to Docker containerization and AWS deployment.
